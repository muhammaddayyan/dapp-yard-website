OUR SERVICES

01. Full Service NFT Development

We will be the one-stop solution for your NFT project. We will help you in building & launching a complete NFT project. Full Service NFT Development includes NFT strategy, NFT design, deployment and maintenance, NFT marketplace development, NFT minting platform development, NFT staking platforms, and NFT auction portals.

02. Full Stack Development

Fullstack Development involves handling both the front end and the back end of a website or application. It includes aspects of project management, database management, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, and potentially many other languages to fully handle all aspects of development.

03. Smart Contract Audit

A Smart Contract Audit is a review of the code of a smart contract to ensure its security and correctness. It identifies any potential issues or vulnerabilities in the code that could be exploited, ensuring the smart contract is safe before it is deployed to production.

04. Smart Contract Development

Smart Contract Development involves writing the code that is stored on the blockchain network. It automatically executes transactions and agreements and controls the transfer of digital currencies or assets between parties under certain conditions.

05. Subgraph / Indexer Development

Subgraph/Indexer Development involves creating and managing APIs that can query data from the blockchain in a more efficient manner. It helps in improving the scalability and speed of blockchain applications.

FAQ

What is the benefit of hiring your agency over freelancers?

When you work with our agency, you get access to an entire team of experts, including developers, designers, strategists, and project managers. We offer increased accountability, transparency, and a reputation to uphold.

What are your agency's business hours?

Our normal business hours are from Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. However, we offer support to our clients outside of these hours as needed.

Do you provide a dedicated project manager for my project?

Yes, we provide a dedicated project manager for each project. The project manager serves as your main point of contact and ensures that the project stays on schedule and on budget

How does your agency handle client communication and collaboration?

We prioritize efficient and transparent client communication. We use project management software to manage tasks and communicate with clients. Our clients get access to our project boards for real-time status updates
